Digital Marketing FAQs

What is digital marketing? ^

Digital marketing is the promotion of products or services using online channels like social media, SEO, email, and paid ads.

How does social media marketing help businesses? ^

It boosts brand awareness, engages with customers, and drives traffic and sales through platforms like Facebook, Instagram, and LinkedIn.

What is influencer marketing? ^

Influencer marketing uses industry experts or social media influencers to promote products/services to their audience for better reach and trust.

What are the key metrics to track in digital marketing? ^

Important metrics include website traffic, conversion rates, engagement, click-through rate (CTR), and return on investment (ROI).
